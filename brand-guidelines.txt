# Zypher Brand Guidelines

## 1. Brand Identity Overview
Modern, sleek, and premium. The interface supports a dual-theme system: a deep, futuristic Dark Theme with metallic accents, and a clean, professional Light Theme with soft greys and sharp typography.

## 2. Color Palette

### Dark Theme (Primary)
The dark theme is the core identity, featuring deep blacks and greys with high-contrast accents.

**Backgrounds**
- **Main Background**: `#151515` (Deep Black)
- **Secondary / Surface**: `#252525` (Dark Grey)
- **Widget Background**: `rgba(44, 45, 47, 0.9)`
- **Glassmorphism Effect**: `backdrop-filter: blur(2px)` to `blur(4px)`

**Primary Accents**
- **Primary Main**: `#EBF3FF` (Ice White/Blue)
- **Primary Weak (50)**: `#E9F9FF`
- **Primary Strong (100)**: `#D7F4FF`

**Status Colors**
- **Success (Green)**: `#4ED79D` (Regular), `#49B482` (Strong)
- **Warning (Gold/Orange)**: `#FFE9C4` (Pale), `#FFCC74` (Vibrant), `#FFF5D0` (Gradient Start)
- **Error (Red)**: `#FF6366`

**Text Colors (Dark Theme)**
- **Primary Text**: `#F7F9FD` (High Emphasis)
- **Secondary Text**: `#E3E8FF` (Medium Emphasis)
- **Muted Text**: `#B9BCCC` (Grey 400), `#E6E7F3` (Grey 100)

**Gradients (Dark Theme)**
- **Gold/Metallic Accent**: `linear-gradient(128.62deg, #FFF5D0 11.02%, #7A766B 93.11%)`
- **Card Gradient**: `linear-gradient(104.01deg, #1F2021 2.88%, #3A3B40 99.58%)`
- **Surface Gradient**: `linear-gradient(129.49deg, rgba(42, 42, 43, 0.9) 3.6%, rgba(60, 60, 60, 0.9) 100%)`

---

### Light Theme
Designed for clarity and trust, primarily used in Authentication and Account Management flows.

**Backgrounds**
- **Main Background**: `#EEEEEE` (Light Grey)
- **Card/Container Surface**: `#FCFEFF` (Off-White)
- **Input Field Background**: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.8)` or `#FFFFFF`

**Text Colors (Light Theme)**
- **Primary Text (Headings/Labels)**: `#323232` (Soft Black)
- **Secondary Text (Inactive/Placeholder)**: `#8C8C8C` (Mid Grey) | `#161B33` (Deep Blue @ 50% opacity)
- **Links / Action Text**: `#3B4A90` (Corporate Blue)

**Borders & Dividers**
- **Vibrant Border**: `#DAE1EA` (Light Blue-Grey)

**Buttons (Light Theme)**
- **Primary Button Background**: `linear-gradient(11.41deg, #252525 5.95%, #707070 102.98%)` (Dark Gradient)
- **Primary Button Text**: `#FFFFFF`
- **Secondary/Toggle Button Background**: `#B1B1B1` (Inactive Grey)

**Logo Gradient (Light Theme)**
- `linear-gradient(88.39deg, #343431 -33.16%, #585952 39.59%, #74716E 100.88%)`

---

## 3. Typography
**Primary Font**: `DM Sans`
- **Weights**: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium), 600 (Semi-bold)
- **Usage**: Main UI text, headers, form labels, buttons.

**Secondary Fonts**
- **SF Pro**: Financial data, numbers, currency.
- **Urbanist**: Hero text, landing page headlines (e.g., "Your Wallet, Reinvented").
- **Roboto**: Occasional body text.

**Type Scale (Reference)**
- **Hero/Display**: 40px (Urbanist, Semi-bold)
- **Page Titles**: 24px (DM Sans, Semi-bold)
- **Subtitles**: 16px (DM Sans, Medium)
- **Body/Inputs**: 14px (DM Sans, Regular)
- **Captions/Labels**: 12px (DM Sans, Medium/Regular)

## 4. UI Elements & Effects

**Border Radius**
- **Cards/Widgets**: `16px` or `20px` (Login Container)
- **Buttons/Input Fields**: `4px` - `5px`
- **Pills/Tags**: `100px`

**Shadows & Blurs**
- **Glass Effect**: `backdrop-filter: blur(2px)` to `blur(4px)` (Used on dark cards).
- **Glows**: Soft gradients behind vectors.

**Input Fields (Light Theme Specifics)**
- **Height**: 40px
- **Border**: 1px solid `#DAE1EA`
- **Background**: White/Transparent White (`rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.8)`).
